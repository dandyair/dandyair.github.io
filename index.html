About us

Dandy Air is a local, family-owned heating, air conditioning and refrigeration contractor. We install and service all major brands of heating and air conditioning equipment for homes, offices and businesses. We are an authorized TRANE® equipment dealer. We service commercial refrigeration equipment, including ice makers, coolers and freezers, for restaurants, convenience stores and offices.

Why choose us?

Our honest approach and dedication to customer satisfaction is unmatched. Home and business owners have come to rely on us for extraordinary service from technicians that often “go the extra mile” to ensure their comfort and convenience.

After the repair you can expect to have the best warranty service available. Most new systems come with a parts warranty of either five (5) years or ten (10) years and a (1) year labor warranty. Warranty service gets top priority with us.

We will do whatever it takes, whenever it is needed. No one cares more about your HVAC problem than Dandy Air.

Our Services

Commercial Refrigeration/AC

We offer installation and repair service for all commercial refrigeration systems. We know your freezers, walk-in and reach-in coolers and ice-makers are critically important to the service you offer your customers and we want to make sure they are in perfect operating order.

We service and install all major commercial brands, including but not limited to:

  • Polar King walk-in freezers
  • Bush floral display coolers
  • Nor-Lake ice cream freezers
  • Flo-Aire vent hoods
  • True reach-in coolers
  • Manitowoc ice makers
  • Kelvinator ice cream dipping cabinets
  • Scotsman under counter ice machines
